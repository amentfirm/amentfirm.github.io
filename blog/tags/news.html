Sentiment News and Tic-tac-toe

February 24, 2023 · One min read
Po
Freelancer
Sing Chun
Freelancer

We used SnowNLP and pysenti to compute two sentiment scores for each HK news that we scrapped daily. Using these scores, we visualize the news in a sentiment square. From this visualization, we can roughly know how happy/sad daily HK news is. On the other hand, we started our game development journal. Inspired by the simple tic-tac-toe implementation by Fellipe Utakausing tailwind and react, we implemented similarly our own tic-tac-toe game.

我哋用咗SnowNLP同pysenti去計每單香港每日新聞嘅情感分數，然後用呢兩個情感分數將新聞顯示係一個二維情感平面上。通過呢個顯示方法，我哋可以大概見到香港每日新聞係開心多定係傷心多。此外，我哋亦踏上寫遊戲嘅旅程。睇到Fellipe Utaka簡單地用tailwind同react去寫井字過三關，我哋都寫左一個相似嘅井字過三關。

HK News on a Sphere

February 10, 2023 · One min read
Po
Freelancer
Sing Chun
Freelancer

We added a cronjob on our server to scrape HK news daily and store them on FireStore. Then we implemented a query to search for the news and used the technique demonstrated by raizensoft (video here) to display HK news on a sphere. To display arbitrary numbers of news evenly on the sphere, we used spherical Fibonacci mapping.

我哋加咗個cornjob係server上每日爬香港新聞同儲存係FireStore上面。然後我哋寫咗個query去搵香港新閒同用咗raizensoft係呢喥示範嘅技巧將每日嘅香港新閒顯示係球上面。我哋用咗spherical Fibonacci mapping將任意數目嘅新閒平均地放係球上面。
